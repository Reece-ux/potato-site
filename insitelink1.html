Preparing the dish

 
A loaded baked potato is a style of cooking a potato that involves baking it in an oven, and then loading it with various toppings such as cheese bacon bits etc.
×

Ingredients:

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 tablespoon ranch seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 6 slices of bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Wash and poke holes in the potatoes with a fork.
  3. Microwave the potatoes until tender.
  4. Cut off the top of the potatoes and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  5. Add butter, sour cream, ranch dressing, ranch seasoning, cheese, bacon, and green onion to the potato flesh and mash it well.
  6. Season the potato skins and add some cheese at the bottom.
  7. Stuff the potato skins with the potato mixture and top with more cheese.
  8. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and golden.
  9. Garnish with sour cream, bacon, and green onion and enjoy!

Potato soup is a soup dish that consists of potatoes and multiple different ingrediants found on loaded baked potatoes such as bacon and sour cream.
×

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 stick (8 tablespoons) unsalted butter, cubed
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream, heated

Instructions:

  1. Put the potatoes in a large pot and cover with cold water.
  2. Generously salt the water and bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Then lower the heat to medium and gently simmer until the potatoes are completely tender when pierced with the tip of a knife, about 30 minutes.
  4. Drain the potatoes well and return them to the pot.
  5. Place the pot over low heat and let any residual water steam off, about 2 minutes.
  6. Mash the potatoes well with a potato masher.
  7. Fold in the butter with a spatula until melted, and then fold in the cream.
  8. Season with salt and pepper.
