The Potato Famine

Potatoes have long been a reliable food source, being easy to farm and quite tasty in some dishes. There was one period of time in Ireland where a dangerous mold made 1 half of the potato harvest unsuitable to be consumed.

Why we eat them today

Potatoes are relatively easy to farm and can be quite good in some dishes. Personally I love potatoes, being one of my favorite side dishes. They also pair well with many different spices, ingrediants, and toppings.
